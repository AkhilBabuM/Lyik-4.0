LYIK.Credentials

The most secure way to receive and share credentials

Credentials are used to identify and describe individuals, such as university degree certificates, property documents, drivers license, etc. They are traditionally issued physically.

Verifiable Credentials are digitally and cryptographically verifiable and traceable to authenticate the party who issued it, to the person who it was issued to. This provides an unprecedented amount of privacy, security and assurance for credentials that we haven't seen before.

LYIK.Credentials is the core of our technology stack that enables governments and enterprises to issue and verify digital credentials based on Web 3.0 technologies, and support consumers to manage these verifiable credentials in their LYIK.Privacy App.

Verifiable credentials are the future of an individual's identity. LYIK is addressing democratization of this concept, clubbed with its innovative form-filling technology to enable millions of users on their mobile devices to store and transact credentials securely with enterprises, thus creating the first ever PII Data Xchange Platform.
