LYIK.Enterprise

A one-point solution for enterprises to create and publish forms, generate QR codes, and much more



While the LYIK.Privacy App is made for consumers, LYIK.Enterprise is made for enterprises. LYIK.Enterprise provides solutions that are secure, global data-privacy compliant and ultra efficient to boost productivity, convenience and better customer experience.

LYIK.Enterprise is a one-point solution for enterprises to create and publish forms, generate QR codes, control marketplace entries, configure payment options, download form submissions, issue and verify digital credentials, and much more.

The architecture of LYIK.Enterprise comes well integrated with Hyperledger Indy, which is a distributed ledger, purpose-built for decentralized identity to offer and manage blockchain-based digital credentials. LYIK.Enterprise is designed to be flexible and integrate with other popular blockchain-based digital credentials platforms such as the one provided by EBSI (European Blockchain Services Infrastructure). All of the above is enabled for enterprises through a user-friendly, feature-rich and sophisticated dashboard on LYIK.Enterprise.
